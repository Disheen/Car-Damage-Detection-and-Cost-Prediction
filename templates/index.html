How it works

Car Damage Detective allows you to upload a picture of your car damage to indepently assess damage location and severity, easing the burden of filing an insurance claim and getting your car repaired.


SNAP

Take a picture of your car damage. For best results, try to focus on one major area of damage (e.g., front, side, or rear) and contain most of the car body in the picture.

SUBMIT

Upload your picture to Car Damage Detective using our handy web app and image upload capabilities.

ASSESS

Convolutional neural networks will identify where and how severely your car is damaged and provide an immediate assessment of your damage

RESULTS

Results can be used to obtain a cost estimate or submit to your insurance company to expedite claims handling

Image tips:

Please upload an image focused on one major area of damage (e.g., front, side, or rear).

Try to contain most of the car in the picture unless it doesn't capture the damage well. If there is more than one major area of damage, you can upload and assess multiple pictures.

The maximum filesize is 10MB.

Only jpg, jpeg, gif and png formats are currently supported.
